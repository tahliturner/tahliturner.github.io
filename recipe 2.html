Tiana’s Beignets from The Princess and the Frog 

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 6 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar

Method

  1. In a bowl, combine the water, sugar and yeast. Let sit for 10 - 15 minutes.
  2. Using a standalone mixer, mix the eggs, evaporated milk and salt until fully combined.
  3. Turn the mixer to low and add the yeast mixture.
  4. Add half the flour and mix until combined.
  5. Add the shortening and continue to mix.
  6. Once combined add the remaining flour and be careful not to over mix.
  7. Take the dough out of the mixer and knead until smooth.
  8. Place the dough back into the bowl, cover with plastic wrap and move bowl to a warm area.
  9. Let dough rise for 2 hours.
  10. Once the dough has finished rising roll it out to 1/4" thick.
  11. Slice the dough into 2" squares.
  12. In a large saucepan, heat the vegetable oil until it reaches a temperature of 160 degrees celsius.
  13. Place a few dough squares into the oil and allow them to cook.
  14. Make sure to turn them so they don't burn.
  15. Once the beignets are done cooking, dust with a layer of icing sugar.
  16. To serve, pile more icing sugar on top. Best served straight away.
